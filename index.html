He worked in different fields and sectors in India and then, with a profound interest and with great ambitions, he figured to move out to United States of America to persue his Masters education. Sujith Babu Gunturu, a native of Tirupathi, Andhra Pradesh, India, born on 06 June 1993, was a steadfast pillar of support to his parents from his early years. His journey led him through various professional endeavors in India before he set his sights on the United States of America to pursue his Master's education. He gained admission to Auburn University at Montgomery for the "Management Information Systems" course, showcasing his keen interest and ambitious spirit. His exceptional listening skills, attention to detail, and his ability to engage with faculty members made him an outstanding student.

Beyond the realm of academia, Mr. Gunturu actively participated in numerous college events, catching the attention of every staff member and earning the role of Secretary of the Indian Student Organization (ISO). Additionally, he served as a Student Assistant in AUM's Office of Global Initiatives, leaving an indelible mark with his memorable contributions to the college community.

In his personal life, Sujith was the source of thought-provoking ideas that enriched the lives of his friends both professionally and personally. His diligence, punctuality, and steadfast principles were admirable traits that defined his character. The void left by his untimely departure is nothing short of a haunting nightmare for all who had the privilege of knowing him.
